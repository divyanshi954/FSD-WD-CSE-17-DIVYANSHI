Colonel Sofia Qureshi

Leader • Pride of the Indian Army

Colonel Sofia Qureshi is an accomplished Indian Army officer and one of the most inspiring women in the Armed Forces. She became known as the **first woman officer to lead an Indian Army training contingent** in a foreign military exercise, showcasing extraordinary leadership and courage. Her journey reflects discipline, commitment, and dedication toward serving the nation.

Major Achievements

  • First Woman Officer to lead an Indian Army contingent in an international exercise.
  • She led the Indian Army’s official press briefing for Operation Sindoor, representing the force with clarity and confidence.
  • Served in various challenging terrains and high-responsibility Army units.
  • Recognized for exemplary leadership, discipline, and operational excellence.
  • Role model for women aspiring to join the Indian Armed Forces.

“Real courage is not the absence of fear, but the strength to overcome it.” Colonel Sofia Qureshi continues to inspire countless young minds to dream big and serve the nation with pride.
