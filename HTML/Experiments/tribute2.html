Sushant Singh Rajput

(1986 – 2020)

Sushant Singh Rajput was an extraordinary actor, dreamer, and a highly intelligent achiever whose journey inspired millions. Born in Patna, he excelled academically, securing a top All India Rank in AIEEE and studying Mechanical Engineering at Delhi College of Engineering. He also cleared multiple engineering entrance exams and was a National-Level Physics Olympiad winner.Known for his curiosity, kindness, and love for science and astronomy, Sushant’s legacy continues to inspire countless hearts

His Achievements

  • Secured a top All India Rank in the AIEEE exam.
  • Studied Mechanical Engineering at Delhi College of Engineering (DCE).
  • Cleared more than 11 engineering entrance exams.
  • National-Level Physics Olympiad winner.
  • Started career as a background dancer in Shiamak Davar’s troupe.
  • Became a household name through the TV show Pavitra Rishta.
  • Impressive Bollywood debut with Kai Po Che.
  • Received massive praise for portraying MS Dhoni in MS Dhoni: The Untold Story.
  • Delivered inspiring performances in Chhichhore, Kedarnath, PK, etc.
  • Had a deep passion for astronomy and owned a telescope to study the stars.
